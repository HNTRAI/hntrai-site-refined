FAQ

Common questions

Who is HNTR AI built for?

HNTR AI is purpose-built for wealth management firms — broker-dealers, RIAs, wirehouses, and wealth platforms — that recruit financial advisors. If your firm spends time and money trying to attract, evaluate, or retain top advisor talent, HNTR AI was built for you.

How is this different from a CRM or recruiting ATS?

CRMs and ATS systems manage your pipeline after you have one. HNTR AI works upstream — identifying who to target, when to reach out, and whether they’re actually a good fit. We complement your existing tools, not replace them.

What does “patent pending” mean?

We’ve filed provisional patents on our core technology, including advisor breakaway prediction (BLIX™), recruiting funnel intelligence, and lifestyle-based compatibility scoring. These filings protect the proprietary methods that power the platform.

Is my data secure?

Yes. HNTR AI is built with enterprise-grade security standards in mind, with compliance guardrails for FINRA, SEC, and state-level recruiting regulations baked into the architecture.
